Starlight Robotics is a volunteer-led educational initiative dedicated to free AI and AI news. Equally, we raise awareness about AI challenges in sustainable development of this world-changing technology and seek potential solutions to those challenges.

Starlight Future Tech News by Starlight Robotics
Launched in December 2024, this project delivers cutting-edge news across some of the most thrilling domains of scientific progress, including AI, Space Exploration, Biotechnology, and Longevity research. Stay informed with updates on the technology shaping tomorrow.

Interdimensional Broadcast Agent (alpha PoC) by Starlight Robotics
Fully-local system for generating radio broadcasts from other dimensions. Once configured, you just click "Change channel", and you are listening to a new show from another bizarre dimension.
