Digital Automation & Robotics, Open to Everyone

Starlight Robotics is a volunteer-led initiative building practical web resources and knowledge products around automation, AI workflows, and robotics.

We currently maintain 8 live sub-projects:

  • Starlight Tools
  • Starlight Longevity
  • Daily Life History
  • Surname Origin
  • Historia Medica
  • Sport Rules
  • Geography Atlas
  • Hobby Portal

Follow our progress on Instagram and join the community at r/starlightrobotics.
